Job Description

Clínica Los Andes is currently seeking a qualified and dedicated Pediatrician to join our dynamic healthcare team in Cali. This is an exciting opportunity for a licensed medical professional who's passionate about pediatric medicine and committed to delivering high-quality care to children and their families.

As a Pediatrician at Clínica Los Andes, you will be responsible for providing preventive and inpatient care to patients from infancy through adolescence. You will assess and treat a wide range of acute and chronic conditions, offer preventive care services, and work closely with parents and caregivers to ensure the physical, emotional, and developmental well-being of every child under your supervision.

Our facility is equipped with modern medical technology and supported by a collaborative team of healthcare providers. The ideal candidate should possess strong diagnostic skills, excellent bedside manner, and a genuine interest in child health. You must be comfortable working in a fast-paced environment and confident making critical medical decisions independently or within a multidisciplinary team.

This position offers the opportunity to practice in one of Colombia's most vibrant cities, with access to an active medical community, professional development resources, and a supportive workplace culture that values empathy, ethics, and medical excellence.

Key Responsibilities

  • Conduct routine check-ups and health assessments.
  • Diagnose and treat common childhood illnesses and injuries.
  • Provide immunizations and monitor developmental milestones.
  • Educate parents on children's health, nutrition, and safety.
  • Collaborate with specialists and support staff when necessary.
  • Maintain accurate and up-to-date medical records.
  • Respond to urgent medical situations as required.
  • Stay updated with the latest medical research and pediatric guidelines.

Required Qualifications

  • Medical degree from an accredited institution.
  • Residency in Pediatrics completed.
  • Current medical license to practice in Colombia.
  • Minimum 2 years of clinical experience preferred.
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
  • Strong ethical standards and a patient-first mindset.
  • Spanish language proficiency required.

What We Offer

  • Competitive salary and performance-based incentives.
  • Supportive work environment with experienced medical staff.
  • Professional development opportunities.
  • Flexible scheduling options.
  • Modern facilities with state-of-the-art medical equipment.
  • Positive culture focused on collaboration and patient care.

How To Apply

Interested candidates should send their CV and a short cover letter outlining their experience and motivation to:

careers@clinicalosandes.com

Early applications are encouraged. Position open until filled.
